Create an intense, cinematic rap music video for "Lose Yourself" featuring Ash, telling a story of struggle, determination, and seizing opportunity across 5-6 minutes.

**Main Character - Ash:**
Ash is a determined rapper with straight floppy hair, 5'6" tall, BMI 29 (stocky build). CRITICAL: Ash's mouth remains completely closed in every scene - no lip movement, maintaining a focused, intense expression. Appearance must be 100% consistent. Clothing: navy blue bomber jacket with white accents, white t-shirt, black jeans, white sneakers.

**Scene 1: Urban Street Struggle**
Ash walks through gritty urban streets at night - rain-slicked pavement, neon signs, graffiti walls. Moving with purpose, intense focus. Street lighting creates dramatic shadows. Capture reflections in windows, determined stride. Moody lighting with cool blues and warm streetlight oranges.

**Scene 2: Intimate Preparation**
Ash in a small, dimly lit room - basement or apartment. Practices, visualizes success. Close-ups of hands, focused expression, intense eyes. Warm, intimate lighting - single source creating depth. Personal moments showing the work behind the dream.

**Scene 3: High-Stakes Performance**
Ash performs in underground rap battle or intimate venue - intense crowd, raw energy. Camera moves through crowd, captures reactions, focuses on Ash commanding space. Handheld shots, low angles, tracking movements. Stage lights cut through smoke, creating dramatic beams.

**Scene 4: Triumphant Stadium Performance**
Ash on massive Super Bowl stage - thousands of fans, explosive energy. Wide shots showing scale, tight shots on commanding presence. Moves with confidence, owns the stage. Bright lighting - stadium lights, colored spotlights, crowd cell phone lights creating sea of stars.

**Scene 5: Extreme Close-Up Intensity**
Very close-up shots of Ash's face - eyes burning with determination, unwavering focus. Dramatic lighting - side-lit for depth, rim lighting, or front-lit. Intercut throughout video at key emotional beats.

**Scene 6: Success Montage**
Quick cuts showing Ash's success - luxury vehicles, exclusive venues, achievement. Maintain grounded, focused demeanor. Rich, aspirational lighting with warm golds and cool blues.

**Visual Style:**
Cinematic, high-contrast. Color palette shifts from cool blues/grays (struggle) to warm oranges/golds (performance) to vibrant colors (success). Fast-paced editing with hard cuts on beats, slow-motion for emotion, dynamic camera movements. Ensure Ash's appearance is completely consistent - same hair, build, clothing, expression, mouth always closed.

**Narrative Arc:**
Story progresses from struggle to preparation to performance to triumph. Each scene builds on previous, showing growth. Editing rhythm matches song intensity - building tension, releasing energy. Close-ups provide intimate connection, wide shots show scale.
